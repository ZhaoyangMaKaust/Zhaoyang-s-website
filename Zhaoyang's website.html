Zhaoyang Ma

Postdoctoral Researcher at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST)

About Me

I obtained my Ph.D. in Civil Engineering from Monash University, mentored by Prof. Ranjith Pathegama Gamage (https://www.monash.edu/engineering/ranjithpathegamagamage). Since July 2022, I have been affiliated with the Division of Physical Science and Engineering at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST). I am a key contributor to a $200,000 project that aims to enhance the resolution of computed tomography (CT) images through the use of advanced deep learning techniques. We have implemented cutting-edge algorithms such as diffusion models and Generative Adversarial Networks for tasks in image super-resolution and segmentation. Our work has gained recognition and has been accepted for presentation at the prestigious SPE Annual Technical Conference and Exhibition (ATCE) for 2023.
